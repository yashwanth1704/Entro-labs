Panchayat Raj Information System (PRIS) maintains a central database of all the taxable entities segregated at various Governing levels. Our Unified and Transparent Tax payment gateway solutions enable citizens to access their Payable tax information online through the portal or mobile app.

With the POS machine collection model at the level of Local bodies allowed for complete digital and cashless solution for tax collection. Apart from being a transparent taxation system, we have developed this product to address multiple extended use cases associated with the functioning of Local Government body like

  • Digitally signed certificates issue
  • Solid waste Management
  • Digital tax payments via online and POS machines
  • Effective usage of Aadhar-based services using Biometric and OTP services
  • Prompt Grievance redressal and RTI application system
  • Micro website for every Panchayat
  • Mobile and RFID based solutions for Sanitation, drinking water monitoring, etc.
