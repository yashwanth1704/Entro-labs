Challenges

The year 2020 has thrown-out daunting challenges for global businesses. It was marked by rise in the occurrence of COVID-19 cases, inordinate pressure on health care systems, total disruption of transport and supplies, slowing-down of economic growth, and almost a stand-still in business activity. All this meant to seek and adopt alternative channels of business, and called for pacing-up of each organization's digital transformation. Importantly, the pace at which the digital transformation process had to be adopted and practiced was never envisioned before.

The transition from legacy to adoption of innovative IT services and further digital transformation process was never so easy. The challenges were daunting and never seen before. Entro Labs rose to the challenges of changed times, with an offer of robust, reliable, scalable and value-driven Enterprise Application Services, Software Product Engineering, Mobility Solutions, Business Process Services, and Industry-specific solutions. Our team of IT architects and business professionals have been leveraging on the right set of people, processes and technologies. This has helped us to enable our clients to sustain customer relationships, and adapt to the market dynamics, and capably managed customer expectations.

  • Data Security : Data breaches and incidents have been rising as attackers are trying to exploit vulnerabilities in the IT systems
  • Skills Gaps : Rapid digital transformation demanded highly skilled people. However, a great disparity was observed between desired skill sets and existing ones
  • Privacy : proliferation of online purchasing and contactless buying has only enhanced the need for businesses to beef up their policies on data protection
  • Business continuity and Disaster recovery : The question is, can businesses continue to function amid disruption caused by unforeseen conditions
  • Agility in IT environments : To achieve early digital transformation process businesses had to look for reliable and innovative SaaS platforms, enterprise systems and multi-cloud environments.
  • Innovative Support : Help desk, network services, storage, and IT support took the front seat. The role of solution providers and MSPs was crucial for creating strategic IT value.
  • Remote working and Infrastructure : Many organizations could sustain and keep afloat largely to their ability to adapt to the remote-working culture. A sea of changes was imparted to the existing infrastructure.
  • Trust in Technology : The heralding of greater privacy protection laws, and more security controls meant that the businesses as well as the customers remain confident of technology as a trustworthy asset.
  • Social Media : Social media provides a platform for connection with people effectively. This is a unique communication platform that offers immediate access to information. For businesses, social media is a powerful tool that helps in prompt customer reach, brand visibility, and helps to sustain customer loyalty.
  • Fake apps and fake content : Fake apps are generally malware in disguise.
